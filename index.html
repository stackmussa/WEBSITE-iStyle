Welcome to i Style Beauty Salon & Training Center

Founded in 1999 by the passionate and skilled Aalia Wajid, i Style has grown into a trusted name in beauty and professional training.

With over two decades of experience and a dedicated team of 25 talented students, we’re committed to delivering top-tier beauty services and empowering the next generation of beauty professionals. At i Style, it's not just about looking good — it's about feeling confident, inspired, and transformed.
